GENRAL FAQ

What is miwhip?

miwhip is a brand new private-hire vehicle app, built on a technology platform that allows city drivers and riders to connect. Providing an on-demand and unique experience for anyone and everyone in London. Available to download on iOS and Android.

How does it work?

The way the app works is simple... riders use the app to request a ride.
A fare estimate is automatically calculated and charged at the end of the journey. This will appear on your miwhip account.
A service fee will be deducted from each journey. Drivers can check their daily earnings and instantly cash out at any time they wish to do so.

What areas does miwhip operate in?

miwhip will be launching in London. We’ll be expanding into other cities very soon.

What platforms is miwhip available on?

miwhip is available for free download on iTunes App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android) supported phones.

What are the different types of rides?

miONE

: For single riders.


miwhip

: Most popular choice. For standard journeys up to 4 passengers.


miwhip+

: Larger vehicle. Up to 6 passengers with more baggage space.


miEXEC

: High-end vehicles for business and pleasure, with a professional driver


miLUX

:Ultimate luxury vehicles, long wheel base for extra leg room and comfort, with a professional and experienced driver.


miWAV

: Wheelchair accessible vehicle, with trained drivers.


miPINK

: For female passengers requesting only female drivers.

DRIVER FAQ

How do I book an appointment?

When you sign up as a new driver via the app, book a date/time by selecting an available day for your chosen service category. Please bring along your vehicle and original documents.

Parking availability near onboarding hubs?

We have allocated spaces for parking for new drivers attending the onboarding.

Where will the drivers onboarding take place?

Acton Hub - Unit 2, Western Avenue Business Park, Mansfield Road, Acton, W3 0BZ, London

Do I need to take a Geo-test?

miwhip is committed to offering riders an excellent experience. During your onboarding day you will be required to pass a geographical test if you have chosen to become a miEXEC or miLUX driver.

Do you need to check my vehicle?

Yes, make sure that you bring your vehicle with you on the onboarding day. Your vehicle won't be activated onto the miwhip platform until it’s checked by our quality inspector.

Can I sign up with the rented vehicle?

You can sign up with an approved rental vehicle. On the day of the sign up, please make sure you bring all original copies of supporting documents.

What are the miwhip requirements?

  • Driving Licence for more than 3 years
  • Private Hire Drivers Licence
  • Private Hire Vehicle Licence
  • National Insurance Number
  • DVLA Check Code
  • MOT (if required)
  • Insurance
  • Vehicle Logbook
  • Insurance Vehicle Schedule (if required)
  • Insurance Driver Schedule (if required)
  • Permission Letter (if required)
  • Hire Agreement (if required)

Do you accept young driver?

We accept drivers aged 21 or over. If you are under 25, we can only accept you if you’ve held a full licence for a minimum of 36 months. You will also have to meet the same eligibility criteria and pass the same checks as all other driver-partners.
UK licence only.

What is the minimum age requirement for the vehicle?

To be able to drive with miwhip, the vehicles must be no older than 5 years old.

How do I change my profile photo?

miwhip does not allow driver to update profile photo due to safety and security reasons. The photo of you will be taken by one of our team members during your on-boarding day. If you wish to change the photo, come in to one of our hubs and we will change your photo.

How do I see my ride history?

Ride history available for you to view at any time. Open miwhip partner app, tap on the menu button, tap on “mijourneys” tab.

I want to change my email address.

To change your email address, please log in to miwhip.com/driver/login. Select Profile > Account Information and edit email address.

How do I get paid?

Driver gets payed a full amount minus miwhip service fee via direct deposit to the nominated bank account.

Do I get paid a waiting time fee?

If you are waiting for the rider for more than two minutes, waiting time is automatically added to the fare.
Waiting charges are as follows:
miwhip - £0.15/minute
miwhip+ - £0.15/minute
miEXEC - £0.30/minute
miLUX - £0.55/minute
miWAV - £0.15/minute
miPINK - £0.15/minute

How can I update my bank details?

To update your bank details information log in to your driver app or use mihwip.com/drivers/login. Select Bank Details from the Menu. ​Please note that updates or changes to your bank account details can delay receipt of your weekly payment by 3-5 business days.

How to become a 5* Driver?

Here’s some key tips and tricks to help drivers improve perform and gain ratings:
Always try to accommodate riders requests.
Dress professionally.
Be well presented.
Greet your riders.
Open, close the door where possible.
Do not use your phone while driving.
Be polite.
Remain calm.
Do not engage in inappropriate conversations with the rider.
If you are late for the pick-up, inform the rider about the situation.
Turn the air-con on when it’s a warm day.
Provide water bottles for passengers.

Once we’ve verified your documents you’ll be invited to the miwhip hub to start the onboarding process to get you signed up onto the miwhip platform.

What is a vehicle logbook?

It is a record of every vehicle registered and its keeper. It’s a registration certificate sent to DVLA for processing.

What is DVLA?

Stands for Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency. It hold drivers and vehicle records in the UK and is appointed by the Govt for registration.

What is a national insurance number?

Is a number registered to your name. It is used for administration and the social security system in the United Kingdom.
