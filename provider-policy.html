5.1 Once you have received your User Number, you can then log on to the App using the User Number and a passcode (chosen by you) and will be enabled to access all of the Services on the App.
5.2 You must use a GPS enabled device to use the Services and it is your responsibility to ensure that your device is compatible with the App. We will not be responsible for any malfunction of your device and we do not provide you with any type of device. 5.3 You are under no obligation to log on to the App at any particular time or for any minimum period. Once you have logged onto the App you will begin to receive the Services and we will send bookings by Riders to you (a “Ride Request”). The Ride Request will inform you the distance to the Rider pick up location and the duration of your journey there. You are under no obligation to accept Ride Requests at any time or to accept a minimum number of Ride Requests. It is entirely your discretion to accept a Ride Request by clicking the “accept Ride Request” function on the App or to decline or ignore Ride Requests.

• 5.4.1 first name;
• 5.4.2 photograph;
• 5.4.3 London PHV licence number;
• 5.4.4 vehicle registration mark and vehicle type;
• 5.4.5 estimated cost of the Ride; and
• 5.4.6 estimated time of arrival at the pick up location.
5.5 Once you arrive at the pick up location the Rider will receive a push notification informing them that you have arrived. We recommend that you wait at least 5 minutes from that time for the Rider to arrive at your PHV (note you may begin charging a late Rider after 2 minutes waiting time, see paragraph 14 below).  
5.6 You may contact a Rider using the function via the App to inform them you are waiting. However, you agree that you will not request a Rider’s personal details or contact a Rider for any reason other than to begin the Ride.  
5.7 Once the Rider is safely in your PHV you may commence the journey by clicking the "Start Ride" function on the App and you will be notified of the Rider’s destination. This commences the Ride and you agree that this creates a legally binding agreement between you and the Rider. You acknowledge that we do not provide the Ride and it is your obligation to the Rider to complete the Ride in the most safe, efficient and professional way.  
5.8 You must not (unless specifically requested by the Rider) allow other persons into your PHV for the Ride.  
5.9 The route that you take to the destination is a matter for your professional discretion, taking account of your knowledge of the area and your driving experience. You should treat each Rider fairly and (unless the Rider specifically requests otherwise) use the fastest or most efficient route. The App may recommend a route but we provide no guarantee that this is the fastest or most efficient route. You may use alternative apps or devices to help you plan your route, or take account of suggestions made by the Rider, but you agree to be ultimately responsible for choosing the route and not to rely on us, the Rider or any app or device in doing so.  
5.10 You acknowledge and agree that you shall not provide any number of Rides in a Saturday to Friday period (a “Week”) in excess of 60 hours per Week. Access to the App will be restricted if you have made 60 hours of Rides in a Week and continuous completion of Rides up to this limit will result in immediate cancellation of access to the Services on the App.

11. PRICE AND PAYMENT

11.1 The App is free to download from the Apple App store or Google Play store.
11.2 You can charge a fare for each Ride you provide to a Rider which you have received via the Services (a “Charge”).
11.3 The estimated Charge (calculated based on a base charge, distance, time and traffic conditions) is shown to you on the App (and to the Rider) when accepting a Ride Request. You will be informed of the calculation of base charges on the App. We take all reasonable care to ensure that the estimated Charge indicated to you is correct. However you acknowledge that this may change due to traffic and duration changes during the Ride. Charges may also change during periods of high demand. The final Charge will be sent to you on completion of the Ride.
11.4 In consideration for our provision of the Services to you, you agree to pay us a payment (“Services Payment") calculated based on each Ride and a percentage of the Charge for that Ride.
11.5 The Services Payment is currently 15% (plus the prevailing rate of VAT at the time) but you acknowledge that we reserve the right to amend this percentage at any time due to any regulatory or market factors. We will provide you with 7 days’ notice of any change to the Services Payment.
11.6 We facilitate payment of the Charge via the App on your behalf as your agent. We shall transfer you all Charges (including Cancellation Charges, see paragraphs 13 and 14) collected in cleared funds from Riders (less the Services Payment) to you each Tuesday in regard to those Charges for the preceding Week.
11.7 If a Rider disputes any Charge amount, we will withhold payment in relation to that Charge until the matter is resolved.
11.8 You acknowledge that the Charge is the only payment you will receive for the Ride. You may not agree a higher or lower Charge with the Rider. All payments must be made by the Rider through their own account on the Rider app and you cannot accept cash payments or tips.
11.9 After the completion of a Ride we will send you an invoice showing the Ride route, the duration of the Ride, the Charge and the Services Payment owed.

13. CANCELLATION BY A RIDER

13.1 A Rider may cancel the Ride prior to your arrival without giving any reason. This may result in a cancellation charge (“Cancellation Charge”) to the Rider as follows:
• 13.1.1 If a Rider cancels within 2 minutes of confirming the Ride - no Cancellation Charge;
• 13.1.2 If a Rider cancels after 2 minutes of confirming the Ride – £5 Cancellation Charge; or
• 13.1.3 If a Rider cancels during the Ride - the Charge will be the cost of the Ride up until that point.



14. CANCELLATION BY YOU

14.1 Once you have accepted a Ride Request you may cancel in the following circumstances;
• 14.1.1 if the you cannot get in touch with the Rider or you cannot confirm their location (for whatever reason) - no Cancellation Charge;
• 14.1.2 if due to unforeseen circumstances such as traffic conditions you cannot get to a Rider location within a reasonable time period - no Cancellation Charge; and
• 14.1.3 if the Rider does not show up at the agreed pick up location within 5 minutes of your arrival - £5 Cancellation Charge (The Charge will commence being calculated from 2 minutes after your arrival at the pick up location until this 5 minute Cancellation Charge).
14.2 We will facilitate the payment of all Cancellation Charges directly to you
